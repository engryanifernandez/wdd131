Zambales, Philippines

The Philippines is an archipelago of over 7,000 islands located in Southeast Asia, bordered by the Pacific Ocean. It is renowned for its rich biodiversity, being home to about 5% of the world's flora and fauna, with a significant number of species endemic to the country. The Philippines is famous for its stunning beaches, vibrant coral reefs, and its unique wildlife, including the Philippine eagle, tarsiers, and its vast variety of tropical fruits. It is also a global leader in coconut production and is known for its beautiful landscapes, from the Banaue Rice Terraces to the Chocolate Hills.
