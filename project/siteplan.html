Project Site Plan of Hazel Diane Fernandez for WDD 131

Site Name

Caleb's Firearm Training


This name was selected because it directly reflects the purpose of the website, which is to showcase firearm training programs led by Caleb. It provides a clear and straightforward representation of the services offered, emphasizing the personal touch and expertise that Caleb brings as the lead instructor. The site aims to attract individuals interested in responsible firearm handling and training by offering a relatable and recognizable brand name. Caleb's passion for firearm safety and dedication to educating others inspired the creation of this site, making the name an ideal fit for promoting his mission and expanding his reach within the community.

Site Purpose

The Caleb's Firearm Training website aims to serve as a comprehensive platform for individuals interested in firearm education and training. It will provide detailed information about the training services available, including specialized courses, safety workshops, and hands-on sessions. The site will also feature staff bios, customer testimonials, and recent updates to build credibility and trust. Additionally, it will offer a convenient booking system, allowing visitors to schedule training sessions directly through the website, making it easier for clients to access quality firearm education and promote safe and responsible firearm practices.

Scenarios

  • What types of firearm training courses are available, and which one is best for beginners?
  • How can I book a training session with Caleb and find out the available dates and times?

Color Schema

Heading Color: #4B0082 (Used for header background and navigation bar)

Heading Font Color: #FFC0CB (Used for font color on the header)

Accent Color: #6A0DAD (Used for accent elements, like borders, icons, or background highlights for specific sections)

Button Color: #D32F2F (Used for call-to-action buttons, alerts, and important notices)

Body Font Color: #333333 (Used for the main body text)

Body Background Color: #E0B3E9 (Used for the body background)

Link Color: #FF69B4 (Used for normal link text and darkened for hovered or active status)

Typography

  • Heading Font: 'Great Vibes', cursive (Used for all headings)
  • Body Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif (Used for all body text)
